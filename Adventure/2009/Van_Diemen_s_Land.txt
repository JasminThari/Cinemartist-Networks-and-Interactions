The film takes place in 1822 in Tasmania and is loosely based on a true story. A group of transported convicts, suffering brutal treatment at the Sarah Island penal settlement on Van Diemens Land, as Tasmania was then known (until 1856) escape into the wilderness in hopes of reaching the settlements to the east.<ref>[http://www.filmschoolrejects.com/reviews/fantastic-fest-review-van-diemens-land-robhr.php Fantastic Fest Review: Van Diemen's Land – Film School Rejects]</ref> Their enthusiasm and bravado soon give way to hunger, which saps their strength and causes them to despair.<ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.smh.com.au/news/entertainment/film/film-reviews/van-diemens-land/2009/09/28/1253989859814.html | title=Van Diemen's Land | first=Paul | last=Byrnes | newspaper=The Sydney Morning Herald | date=28 September 2009 | access-date=23 May 2010 }}</ref> Former urban dwellers, the English, Irish and Scottish convicts realise that not only are they lost,<ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.thevine.com.au/entertainment/reviews/van-diemen%27s-land-_-review.aspx | title=Van Diemen's Land – review | first=Anthony | last=Morris | date=22 September 2009 | publisher=TheVine | access-date=4 October 2009 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091002104122/http://www.thevine.com.au/entertainment/reviews/van-diemen's-land-_-review.aspx | archive-date=2 October 2009 | url-status=dead}}</ref> but they do not even know how to hunt or fish. Hunger and despair forces the group to switch to cannibalism, and the band is separated by a difference in opinion on this. Some of the group members separate from the group and walk to their imminent death. The men do all in their power to keep moving, watch their back and avoid sleep, lest they be the next meal.<ref>[http://f3a.net/van-diemen%27s-land,film,1508.html Van Diemen's Land (2009) – Fantasy FilmFest Archiv]</ref> <ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.vandiemensland-themovie.com/ |title=Home |website=vandiemensland-themovie.com |access-date=1 November 2010 |archive-date=10 October 2010 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101010002908/http://www.vandiemensland-themovie.com/ |url-status=dead }}</ref>
