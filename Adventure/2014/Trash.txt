''Trash'' follows three Brazilian street teenagers in Rio de Janeiro; Raphael, Gardo, and Rat (Jun-Jun) who spend their time picking through litter in the hope of finding useful waste. One day they discover a wallet whose contents bring them into conflict with the brutal local police force as they find themselves unlikely whistleblowers in a city rife with corruption.<ref>{{cite news|title=Stephen Daldry and Richard Curtis pick up Trash|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2011/apr/06/stephen-daldry-richard-curtis-trash|access-date=22 July 2014|newspaper=The Guardian|date=6 April 2011}}</ref>
