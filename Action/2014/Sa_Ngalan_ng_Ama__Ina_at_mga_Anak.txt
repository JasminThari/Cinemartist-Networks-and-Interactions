Set in the turbulent 1970s, the movie is about Ongkoy, a former leader of the reputable anti-communist vigilante group Kuratong Baleleng. Regarded as the “Robinhood” of Ozamiz in Mindanao, his strong views on family and patriotism remains to be the stuff of legend in Filipino culture up to the present times.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://starcinema.abs-cbn.com/movies/sa-ngalan-ng-ama-ina-at-mga-anak |title=Sa Ngalan ng Ama, Ina at mga Anak &mdash; Star Cinema |website=ABS-CBN}}</ref>
