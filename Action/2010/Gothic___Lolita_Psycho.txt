Yuki (Rina Akiyama) lives with her father Jiro (Yurei Yanagi) and her mother Kayako (Fumie Nakajima) in Tokyo. Their peaceful lives are disturbed when a group of assassins slaughter Kayako and injure Jiro. Yuki seeks revenge by dressing herself in Gothic Lolita fashion and killing off the assassins.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ponycanyon.co.jp/intl/mp_pc/archive/gothic_lolita.html|title=Gothic & Lolita Psycho|accessdate=July 29, 2011|work=Pony Canyon International Licensing}}</ref>
