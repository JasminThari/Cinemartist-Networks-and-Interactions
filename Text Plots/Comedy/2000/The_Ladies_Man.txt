Leon Phelps (also known as the "''Ladies Man''") was a ''Saturday Night Live'' character played by Tim Meadows during the 1990s. The sketch was that of a broadcast program in which Phelps, a young, suave black man, would give dubious romantic advice and lovemaking tips.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.lycos.com/info/tim-meadows.html |title=Tim Meadows |publisher=Lycos.com |date=1961-02-05 |access-date=2010-11-05}}</ref>  The Ladies Man openly proclaimed that he would court any woman at all including skanks, providing the woman weighs no more than 250 pounds.  A night of romance would generally revolve around a bottle of Courvoisier.
