A homage to end of times Christian horror movies of the 1970s, the story follows the crisis of faith of an unemployed alcoholic and a pregnant, cocaine snorting, pill popping teenager. Two demons try to claim their souls, while heavenly missionaries try to set the damned heroes on the path of redemption.<ref>[http://www.slantmagazine.com/film/review/satan-hates-you/5207 Slant Magazine]</ref>
